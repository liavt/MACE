Detailed Description

Special buffer that excels at asynchronous pixel transfer operations.

The PixelUnpackBuffer is fast at reading pixels while the PixelPackBuffer is fast at writing pixels.

Not to be confused with a FrameBuffer or Texture.

See also
https://www.opengl.org/wiki/Pixel_Buffer_Object
